# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

## Do’s
1. Use professional language in posts and comments.
2. Keep your profile information accurate and up-to-date.
3. Share useful academic or professional content.
4. Respect privacy settings and personal boundaries.
5. Think before posting — ensure it reflects positively on you.

## Don’ts
1. Avoid sharing inappropriate or offensive content.
2. Don’t post confidential information about college or peers.
3. Avoid excessive personal details or photos.
4. Don’t engage in arguments or online bullying.
5. Don’t click or share suspicious links.
