Prevention Checklist:

# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Tips for Students

1. Always verify email senders before clicking links.  
2. Hover over links to check URLs before opening them.  
3. Enable **two-factor authentication (2FA)** on all accounts.  
4. Use strong, unique passwords for each platform.  
5. Never share personal or banking information via email.  
6. Avoid downloading attachments from unknown sources.  
7. For UPI/online payments:  
   - Verify recipient details carefully.  
   - Use official bank apps or UPI apps only.  
8. Regularly monitor bank accounts and report suspicious activity immediately.  
9. Report cybercrime incidents to:  
   - **National Cyber Crime Portal:** [cybercrime.gov.in](https://www.cybercrime.gov.in)  
   - **Helpline:** 1930
